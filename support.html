Getting Started

What is Finnote?

Finnote is a simple daybook and finance app for Indian businesses and personal use. Track cash in/out, create invoices, and export reports ‚Äî all offline.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Finnote works completely offline with no login required.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. We never have access to it.

Features

Business Mode vs Personal Mode

  • Business Mode: Track cash flow, create GST invoices, export to Tally
  • Personal Mode: Track daily expenses, categorize spending

You can switch between modes in Settings.

How do I create an invoice?

  1. Go to Invoices tab
  2. Tap + to create new invoice
  3. Add customer, items, and GST rate
  4. Save and share PDF

How do I backup my data?

  1. Go to More ‚Üí Backup & Restore
  2. Tap "Create Full Backup"
  3. Save the backup file

Troubleshooting

Voice entry not working?

  • Make sure microphone permission is granted
  • Speak clearly with amount and description
  • Example: "Received 500 rupees from customer"

Export failed?

  • Check storage space on your device
  • Try exporting a smaller date range
  • Restart the app and try again
